How to review (please read)
  1. Enter your name or email below so we know who provided each ID.
  2. For each card: look at the close-up (drag the opacity slider to see the mask fill; the cyan outline and amber box mark the segmented organism). Click Show whole frame for context.
  3. Pick the matching code. If it is one of the highlighted target species, just click it. Otherwise choose Something else and find it by group. If you cannot tell, leave “I don’t know” selected or click Skip.
  4. Set confidence (defaults to High).
  5. When done (or after any number of cards), click Export CSV at the bottom and email the downloaded file to the addresses shown. Your progress is saved in this browser, so you can come back later.
