How to review (please read)
  1. For each card, look at the close-up (drag the opacity slider to reveal the mask fill; the cyan outline and box mark the segmented organism). Click Show whole frame for context.
  2. Pick the matching code. If it is one of the highlighted choices, just click it. Otherwise choose Something else and find it by group (coral, algae, sponge, gorgonian, substrate, and more). If you cannot tell, leave “I don’t know” selected or click Skip.
  3. Set confidence (defaults to High).
  4. When done (or after any number of cards), click Export CSV at the bottom and email the downloaded file to the address(es) shown. Your progress is saved in this browser, so you can stop and come back later.
